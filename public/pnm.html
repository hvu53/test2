Jiff is a digital health technology company that enables healthcare organizations to engage in collaborative, connected relationships with the consumers they serve. Using Jiff's HIPAA compliant Platform, Jiff's customers can quickly deploy digital health applications via the web or mobile devices that combine the best of the consumer Web with the best of their clinical content.


Founded in 2010 in Palo Alto, California, Jiff is lead by a world-class team of healthcare and consumer-internet leaders. Jiff is privately held and venture backed.


As described in the Project NOT ME "Terms of Use", Jiff is a "website-related party" and is collaborating with Optum on the design, development and maintenance of the Project NOT ME portal.
